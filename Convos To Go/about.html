Our Company

ConvosToGo is an online service that connects people of all kinds to specialized experts in a variety of fields. Our goal is to share and spread knowledge and connection in a way that feels fresh, but is also the simplest of them all: human interaction.

Our Service

To interact and develop connections, we offer three types of subscriptions: Instant Message, Video Chat, and Face-to-Face conversation. Once a client has selected their preference and expressed their interests, they are free to browse our extensive roster of experts who can then be contacted to schedule meetings.

Our Start

ConvosToGo began with three college students who wondered if humanity’s innate need for sharing knowledge, interest, & experience could become something more. Starting on campus at Rutgers University – Camden, they pooled the resources around them for the first ever group of experts and the project has only grown from there.

Our Founders

Clara Cribb

A Fine Arts major with a natural eye for authenticity, aesthetic and the little details, Clara co-founded the project recognizing it as an opportunity for diverse communities with dissimilar interests to find like-minded characters to learn from.

Anthony See

A Computer Science major with a background in Graphic Design, Anthony has always appreciated when things fit into their rightful place, joining ConvosToGo with the belief that linking people together based on what they enjoy is the best design of all.

Reet Taylor

Majors in both Digital Media and Sociology, Reet has always felt that inclination towards helping others and finding new ways in bring people together. As a co-founder of the project, the opportunity emerged in making that connection happen.
